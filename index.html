About Career Services

Career Services counsels students and alumni as they explore career directions. Since career development is preparation for life, our mission is to help you gain self-understanding and connect your interests, values and skills with knowledge about careers and life beyond Swarthmore College. In support of that mission, we build relationships with faculty, colleagues and students' families, and serve as a resource for employers and graduate schools.

Swarthmore College Career Services acts only as an intermediary between employers posting job opportunities and candidates searching for job opportunities. We do not verify the authenticity or legitimacy of the jobs or internships that are posted. The ultimate responsibility for researching a potential employer lies with the applicant. We have no control over the quality, safety, or legality of the jobs or resumes posted, the truth or accuracy of the listings, the ability of employers to offer job opportunities to candidates, or the ability of candidates to fill job openings. We recommend that all job seekers learn about protecting themselves in the job search.
